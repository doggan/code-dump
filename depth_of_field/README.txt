Keys:
    * Press 1-4 to switch between the different parameters.
    * Use the bracket keys [] to increment/decrement the values.
    * Press 5 to toggle the DoF effect on/off.
    * Press 6 to toggle the near DoF effect on/off.
    * Press 7 to toggle the far DoF effect on/off.
    * Press 8 to toggle on/off debug depth window.
